Angry Apple

When vegetables pluck you from your home tree and drag you deep under the Earth, what on Earth are you to do?. Get your revenge of course! Angry Apple is a game about killing and beating the absolute s*** out of vegetables and having fun doing it. Taking much insparation from the Doom universe I decided I wanted to make a 2D platformer metroidvania called Angry Apple to challenge other games like it. What makes this game unique is it's intricate approach to making the combat system smooth and engaging while also keeping a comedic theme throughout.

Features I have so far: Platforming physics, hazards and traps, broccoli enemy and animations, player attack animations, player hurt and death animations, working ladders, and rising water.
